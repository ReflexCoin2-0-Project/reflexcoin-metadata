What is RFX2 used for?

ReflexCoin 2.0 (RFX2) is an experimental token built on the Solana blockchain, designed to explore how emotion, narrative, and collective attention can influence perceived digital value.

The supply is permanently fixed at 2,000,000 tokens. There is no minting, no inflation, no venture capital, and no insider allocation.

RFX2 is not a utility token, not a governance token, and not a promise of profit. It is an open experiment built in public.
